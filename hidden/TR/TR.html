TRANSISTOR RIOT

We shoot individual passes of each band member (and maybe some combinations) across a day or two (under sun, moon, and twilight), steadily circling them (like holding down a thumbstick). Under the precision of the move, we can transition between passes seamlessly, percieving the inprecise layers of reality with the unreal omniscience of cyberspace.
