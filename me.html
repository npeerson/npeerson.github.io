i am a filmmaker on unceded coast salish territory in vancouver, bc.

my work has screened at such festivals as the vancouver queer film festival, the imagine|native film + media arts festival, the canadian independent film festival, the tasveer south asian film festival and the feminist border arts festival.

my sensibilites are rooted in a heightened naturalism. i seek to capture authentically with a minimal footprint.

my favourite colour is red

my pronouns are they, them, theirs
